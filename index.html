Top three activities to do in Busan

Temples

The water temple is a great place to visit. There are also some nice beaches along the coast.

Street food

Street food is a must. It isn’t just cheap. It is also very tasty, and can be spicy.

Traditional drinks

A night out on the town would not be complete with out a glass of soju or Makkoli.
